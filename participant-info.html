Participant information sheet

Title of Study

Referential Communication Game

What is the study about?

You have been invited to particpate in a research study looking at how people play a communication game. This study is being conducted by University College Dublin researchers in the School of Information and Communication Studies. The study is being conducted by University College Dublin's School of Information and Communication Studies. The study is being led by Dr. Benjamin Cowan.

Research Activity:

You will be mathced with a partner and take turns naming or clicking on images. After you have completed the game, you will be asked to complete a short demographic questionnaire.

Time Commitment:

The study should take no longer than 30 minutes to complete in its entirety.
You may withdraw from the study at any point If you do decide to withdraw from the study, the data collected to that point will be deleted. If you have any questions as a result of reading this information sheet, please feel free to contact the research team.

Data Collection/Protection:

Your data will be de-identified. All data collected in the experiment session will be associated with your AMT Turker ID and will not be attached to your name in any way. The data collected will be stored securely in compliance with GDPR regulations.
